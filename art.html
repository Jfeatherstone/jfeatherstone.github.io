Scientific Graphics

As a part of my work as a researcher, I regularly create scientific figures or graphics for presentations, papers, or posters. My main tools are generally 2D vector art (Inkscape) and 3D modeling (Blender), though I also hand draw some images every now and again (see #SketchYourScience below).
For a more complete collection of illustrations, please see this repo: Jfeatherstone/science-art!

#SketchYourScience

#SketchYourScience is an initiative from AGU lead by Irene Crisologo and Mj Riches encouraging scientists to take a creative approach to scientific communication through cute sketches and drawings!
Many of the images on this site are inspired by this initiative, and a more complete collection of my sketches can be found in this repo: Jfeatherstone/sketch-your-science.
