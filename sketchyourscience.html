#SketchYourScience

#SketchYourScience is an initiative from AGU lead by Irene Crisologo and Mj Riches encouraging scientists to take a creative approach to scientific communication through cute sketches and drawings!
All of the images on this site are inspired by this initiative, and a more complete collection of my sketches can be found in this repo.
If you are interested in using any of these images for your presentation, website, or whatever else, please go right ahead! I ask that you attribute me as the original creator, but other than that, no stipulations.
